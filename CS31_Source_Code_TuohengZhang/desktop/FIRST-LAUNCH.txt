CS31Preview - First Launch Guide
=======================================

---- WINDOWS USERS: READ THIS FIRST ----

Before launching CS31Preview.exe for the first time, you must download
the ~4 GB AI model manually:

  1. Make sure Python is installed (https://www.python.org/downloads/).
     During installation, check "Add Python to PATH".
  2. Double-click "download_sd_model.bat" in this folder.
  3. Wait for it to print "Download complete!" (10-30 minutes).
  4. Then double-click CS31Preview.exe to launch the app.

If you skip step 2-3, the app will show a download dialog that gets stuck.

-----------------------------------------

---- macOS USERS ----

This app is not signed with an Apple Developer ID, so macOS Gatekeeper
may show an "unidentified developer" warning and block a normal double-click launch.

First-time workaround:
  1. Drag CS31Preview.app into Applications (/Applications).
  2. Control-click or right-click the app, then choose "Open".
  3. A warning may appear stating that the application cannot be opened because it is from an unidentified developer. This is expected. Click "Done" to close the dialog. 
  4. Go to "System Setting" on your Mac computer, on the left pannel, scroll down and click "Privacy & Security". 
  5. Go to the right side panel of "Privacy & Security", scroll to "Security" section, you can see the message that "CS31Preview was blocked to protect your Mac", then click "Open Anyway". This is a one-time action, once you clicked "Open Anyway" you do not need to repeat the same action every time you open "CS31Preview" application after this.
  6. On the pop-up window, click "Open Anyway" again, and enter your password for your Mac computer.
  7. Then the "CS31Preview" will be opened automatically, you can double-click this icon next time to directly open the app, without going through the above steps again.

After this first approval, double-click launch should work normally.

---

First-run behavior:
  - The app will show a "Download Model" dialog and download about 4 GB once.
  - By default, it uses the hf-mirror.com mirror and usually finishes in 5-10 minutes.
  - After download, the model is stored at:
    ~/Library/Application Support/CS31Preview/models/
  - Future launches can run offline using the local model.

---

Generated result save location:
  ~/Pictures/CS31Preview/
  (You can also choose another location in the Save dialog.)

Privacy:
  * All computation runs locally on your Mac.
  * Photos are not uploaded to any server.
  * You can delete ~/Library/Application Support/CS31Preview/ at any time to clear local data.
